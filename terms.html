The short version

Use LOFO.AI to find and return lost items. Don't use it to claim things that aren't yours, harass other users, or submit false reports. Doing the right thing is the whole point. If that's not why you're here, this isn't the app for you.

The service

LOFO.AI provides a platform that uses AI to match found items with lost item reports and coordinate their return. We are a coordination service — we do not take custody of items, guarantee returns, or act as a shipping or logistics provider.

Your responsibilities as a finder

  • Submit accurate photos and descriptions of items you genuinely found
  • Only submit items you are willing to return to their rightful owner
  • Follow through on handoff arrangements you agree to
  • Do not submit items you intend to keep regardless of matching

Your responsibilities as a loser

  • Submit accurate descriptions of items you genuinely lost
  • Provide a secret detail that only you would know — this is how we protect you
  • Do not attempt to claim items that are not yours
  • Multiple failed verification attempts will lock your claim and flag it for review

Tips and payments

Tips from losers to finders are voluntary. LOFO.AI takes a platform fee on tips processed through the service. Tipping is entirely optional — finders are civic volunteers, not contractors, and no finder is entitled to a tip as a condition of return.

All payments are processed by Stripe. LOFO.AI does not store payment card information.

SMS messaging

By tapping "Send Code" or "Confirm Phone Number" in the LOFO app, you consent to receive SMS messages from LOFO including one-time verification codes and match alert notifications. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out at any time. Reply HELP for support.

Message frequency varies based on account activity, typically 1–3 messages per item submission. We do not send marketing messages.

For support, email support@lofoapp.com or use the Support section in the LOFO app.
